Rates & Details

FACILITIES LakeShore Campground features 30 full-service RV sites, 5 dedicated tent sites, a one-unit boutique hotel, and multiple vehicle storage sites.
RV SITES The RV site price is $60.00 per day, which includes power, water, sewer, WIFI, use of the washrooms, and use of the showers.
TENT SITES The tent site price is $50.00 per day, which includes power, water, WIFI, use of the washrooms, and use of the showers.
HOTEL Our one-unit, 900-square-foot hotel suite is $150 per day plus a per-stay $75 cleaning fee, and features a full kitchen, full washroom, separate bedroom with a queen-size bed, flex room with a queen-size futon sofa bed, living room with a double sofa bed and HDTV, air conditioning, private gigabit WIFI, and a view of the lake.
STORAGE SITES We also have vehicle and trailer storage sites of various sizes available. Please contact us for rates.
POOL & WATERSLIDES The pool and waterslides are permanently closed.
CAMPGROUND MAP Click here to download a detailed map of the campground, prepared by Bearcat Jones himself.
CONCESSION We sell bagged ice, pop, water, and ice cream bars at the SunTerra Snackeria.
CHECK IN & OUT TIMES Check-in time is 1:00 PM. Check-out time is 11:00 AM.
CAMPFIRES Wood burning and coal burning campfires are prohibited in Osoyoos, but portable propane fire pits are allowed.
PETS We accept quiet, friendly, well-behaved cats and dogs. Dogs must be kept on a leash at all times on campground property.
RULE: Excessive noise, abusive conduct, or any behavior that interferes with the recreation and comfort of other guests is not allowed at SunTerra. Any violation of these rules may result in the perpetrator being asked to leave the campground without refund.
QUIET TIME In consideration of other guests, loud music, conversation, and partying are prohibited between 10:00 PM and 9:00 AM.
RESERVATIONS To make a reservation, click the "BOOK A VISIT" button above, or email us at info@xxxxxx.com, or call or text us at xxxxxxxx.
RESERVATION DEPOSIT: All bookings require a $50.00 reservation deposit, which can be paid using our secure online payment system. Email us at info@sunterracampground.com for details.
CANCELLATION POLICY If a reservation is cancelled at least 10 days before arrival, the deposit, minus a $20 administration fee, will be refunded. If a reservation is cancelled less than 10 days before arrival, the deposit will not be refunded.
PAYMENT METHODS We accept in-person and secure online payments using Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay, Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover, and Interac Debit.
