Rates & Details


FACILITIES Sunterra Campground features 30 full-service RV sites, 5 dedicated tent sites and multiple vehicle storage sites.
RV SITES The RV site price is $60.00 per day, which includes power, water, sewer, WIFI, use of the washrooms.
TENT SITES The tent site price is $50.00 per day, which includes power, water, WIFI, use of the washrooms.
All Pricing are for 2 people per site /per night. Extra person - $10 per site /per night. Maximum capacity - 5 people.
STORAGE SITES We also have vehicle and trailer storage sites of various sizes available.
Boat or extra vehicle parking rates per night: weekday - $10, weekend -$15, long weekend -$20
POOL & WATERSLIDES The pool and waterslides are permanently closed.
CAMPGROUND MAP Click here to view a detailed map of the campground.
CHECK IN & OUT TIMES Check-in time is 1:00 PM. Check-out time is 11:00 AM.
CAMPFIRES Wood burning and coal burning campfires are prohibited in Osoyoos, but portable propane fire pits are allowed.
PETS We accept quiet, friendly, well-behaved cats and dogs. Dogs must be kept on a leash at all times on campground property.
RULE: Excessive noise, abusive conduct, or any behavior that interferes with the recreation and comfort of other guests is not allowed at Sunterra. Any violation of these rules may result in the perpetrator being asked to leave the campground without refund.
QUIET TIME In consideration of other guests, loud music, conversation, and partying are prohibited between 10:00 PM and 9:00 AM.
